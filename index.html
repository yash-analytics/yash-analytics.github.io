About

Hi, I am Yash, I am driven by the thrill of architecting solutions that not only process information but do so efficiently at scale! As a Data Engineer with 3+ years of experience, I thrive on building solutions that not only solve problems but also create value. At Yantra Inc., I led a cross-functional team to implemented scalable architectures and data integration ETL pipelines that substantially improved forecasting accuracy and system efficiencies. My work at Aldo Group and McKesson Canada involved integrating billions of interactions from Google Analytics, CRM and APIs using AWS Glue and PySpark.


In my free time, I love cooking, exploring the outdoors and cities, and running along the St. Lawrence River. I hiked Mount Sutton in the snow and in the summer, taking in the stunning views. I'm always on the lookout for the best trails and good recipes, so feel free to share the same with me!
